Hello

I had a similar problem with Texlive2009 -vanilla installation-
on Ubuntu Karmic. Latex+dvips was ok but pdflatex not !
I tried a latex document with lmodern and a second one with MinionPro
to understand.
It was a directory problem. Something changed with texlive2009.
I copy the files *.enc to
/usr/local/texlive/2009/texmf-dist/fonts/enc/dvips/MinionPro/

and I put the MinionPro.map in the texlive/2009/(....) directory
and not in the texmf-local.
Perhaps it is not complete.
Ok it is not very clean, but it works. 



O.G.
