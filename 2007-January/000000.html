Am 31.12.2006 um 18:08 schrieb Marco Kuhlmann:

> Getting back to my own mail, it seems that the /foo1-glyphs are
> actually missing from the virtual fonts: as an example, the LaTeX
> command \varphi does not yield any output. Could it be that these
> glyphs have different names in the new versions of the font? Would it
> be worth to play around with mn-oml.enc (which seems to handle that
> part)? What is the best way to test whether the glyph mentioned in
> the encoding file actually makes it into the the virtual font?

Getting back to myself once more: I have now found out that the  
missing glyphs were caused by the broken installation of otftotfm  
that is shipped with Gerben Wierda's latest i-Package. The program as  
it is installed in this package cannot find the glyphlists that are  
used by otftotfm to resolve non-standard glyph names -- such as  
theta1. Re-compiling with a working installation of otftotfm seems to  
work fine!
