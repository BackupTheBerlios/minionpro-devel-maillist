One has to bear in mind that the new metrics are all generated from  
font version 2.000, so they may differ from the v1.00x ones slightly.
I just tested on a miktex 2.5 system with MnSymbol 1.4 a 750 pages book
  1) a setup with the old MinionPro release (v1001 metrics)
  2) RC 3 (v2000 metrics)
result: one different page break on one page, but I didn't spot a  
single different line break.

Inspecting
   otftotfm -e ../enc/mn-t1.enc -p -fkern -fliga ../../fonts/otf/ 
MinionPro/v2000/MinionPro-Regular.otf stand2000
   otftotfm -e ../enc/mn-t1.enc -p -fkern -fliga ../../fonts/otf/ 
MinionPro/v1001/MinionPro-Regular.otf stand1001
   otftotfm -e ../enc/mn-t1.enc -p -fkern -fliga ../../fonts/otf/ 
MinionPro/v1000/MinionPro-Regular.otf stand1000

and then diffing the vpl there is no difference in CHARWD in 2000 and  
1001, just a few between 2000 and 1000. But CHARHT/CHARDP do vary in  
many cases (but only very slightly).
