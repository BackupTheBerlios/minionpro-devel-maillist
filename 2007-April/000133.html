     Dear list,

some of you may be interested in the following change to MnSymbol. I  
recently wrote a text about mathematical structures that are defined  
by two partial orders, for which I use the symbols \trianglelefteq  
and \preceq. Since these two symbols often occur closely together, I  
found it a little distracting that the eq-parts of the two symbols  
appear at different heights. Therefore, I prepared the following  
patch against mnsymbol, which moves the eq-line of the \preceq symbol  
to the same y-axis as the eq-line of the \trianglelefteq-Symbol (and  
the \leq symbol).

     Best,
     Marco
