> opticals, which I only have available in v1.01. At any rate, ligatures,

I don't have pdftex 1.40 installed. But concerning glyph names and thus 
automatic cmap generation, font versions v1.001 and v2.000 do not differ 
with respect to ligatures and small caps. An example where they differ 
are proportional lining figures and tabular text figures.

IIRC there was some noise on the pdftex mailing list that a text file 
required for automatic cmap generation was missing in one of the pdftex 
1.40 releases. Quite possible this is the cause to your problem.
