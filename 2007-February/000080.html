Hi,

> At any rate, ligatures, letterspaced small caps and others are not
> selectable when I compile with pdfTeX 1.40.3.

pdfTeX's new ToUnicode feature doesn't seem to be enabled by default.
Did you follow the usage guidelines at
http://sarovar.org/tracker/?func=detail&atid=495&aid=580&group_id=106 ?

I can search all of the ligatures (or convert them to text) in the
following document if compiled with pdftex-1.40.3 (TeXLive 2007) and
1.001 fonts:

\input glyphtounicode
\pdfgentounicode=1
\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{MinionPro}
\begin{document}
fft fj ffj ff fi fl ffi ffl ft Th
\end{document}
