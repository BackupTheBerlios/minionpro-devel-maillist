Sorry for this premature post, I think this is a feature contained in
the MinionPro-FontDef.sty package:

\Mn at AddToConfig{fontset/weight}{fullfamily/m}{
        <-6>    otf* [weight=Semibold]
       <6-8.5>  otf* [weight=Medium]
     <8.5->     otf* [weight=Regular]
}

Thanks,

Bernhard.
