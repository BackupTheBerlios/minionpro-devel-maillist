How about  using



\documentclass{article}

\usepackage{MinionPro}
\DeclareMathSymbol{v}{\mathalpha}{letters}{"1D}

\begin{document}

$ u v w \boldsymbol{u} \boldsymbol{v} $

\end{document}




That gives the intended result for me. However, \mathbf{v} yields a  
bold fl ligature...
